Earth Sim

Early SNU universe prototype

Disclaimer

This project precedes all versions of SNU by over 1 year. This project was originally not related.

Description

Earth simulator was an early HTML concept made by Sean when he first got into HTML. This was when Seans HTML knowledge was extremely limited.

Most of development went towards adding in states and cities from the United States, and it didn't go further than that.

First version: 1 (June 12th 2016)

Last version: 30 (July 24th 2016)
